About Nucleobytes

Nucleobytes is home to a number of innovative Mac and iOS applications for scientists and researchers, including Apple Design Award-winning applications 4Peaks and EnzymeX. It also used to be the home of Papers app, the reference manager for Mac and iOS that won an Apple Design Award as well. While Papers has left its parental home, Nucleobytes today is a driving force behind the two newborn applications Findings and Manuscripts.



Mek en Tosj: Alexander Griekspoor (@mekentosj) and Tom Groothuis (@ttwimlex)


  • 4Peaks helps molecular biologists to visualize and edit their DNA trace files, made by Nucleobytes.com. We write innovative Mac and iOS software for scientists and researchers, including award-winning applications like EnzymeX, Findings and Manuscripts.

  • The original version of 4Peaks was released in February 2004, written by Alexander Griekspoor (Mek) and Tom Groothuis (Tosj).

  • 4Peaks is a freeware program, made possible by support of the Netherlands Cancer Institute (NKI) in Amsterdam.



  • Engineering & Human Interface Design: Alexander Griekspoor

  • Suggestions, reactions or comments: All feedback is highly appreciated! Tell us what you think of our program, what you use it for, and what you would like to see in a next version. We plan to incorporate as many features as possible. Please visit our website by selecting "Feedback on 4Peaks" from the 4Peaks menu. Alternatively, you can directly contact us at feedback@nucleobytes.com.

  • With special thanks to: We would like to thank the helpful people of the Apple developer community.

  • For more information about 4Peaks, read the credits you find by going to "About 4Peaks" under the "4Peaks" menu.


More Apps for Science

4Peaks is not the only Nucleobytes program, we have also made:

  • EnzymeX. The complete restriction enzyme program, with recognition sites, overhangs, buffer compositions, digestion suggestions and other information regarding DNA digestion.

  • Findings. Your labjournal, reinvented. A worthy successor of LabAssistant.

  • Manuscripts. Make it your best work, a writing tool for scientists and researchers.

  • Oher ideas? Do you have ideas or suggestions regarding 4Peaks or other matters, please send us your feedback at: feedback@nucleobytes.com.
